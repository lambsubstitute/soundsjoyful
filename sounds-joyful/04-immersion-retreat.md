## The Problem You Solve

Leadership retreats and company away days often pack too much in.

Agendas are full. People leave exhausted rather than restored.

What senior teams need is space: to breathe, to connect, to step away from the pace of daily work and experience something genuinely nourishing.

---

## What the Experience Is Like

For organisations wanting to create a truly memorable experience for their people, the Sounds Joyful Retreat offers a full day of immersive wellbeing in a beautiful retreat setting.

This carefully curated experience combines:

- **Rhythm and drumming sessions** — Collective energy and connection
- **Collective singing** — The power of shared voice
- **Immersive sound baths** — Deep rest and nervous system reset
- **Inspiring talks** — Jo and Emma share insights on connection, creativity and wellbeing
- **Guided reflection** — Space to integrate the experience

Participants move through a rich programme that restores energy, builds trust and reconnects people to what matters.

The result is a rare opportunity for teams to step away from the pace of daily work and experience something that stays with them long after the day itself.

## The Ladder of Engagement

Some organisations start with the Reset Experience, love it, and then book the Connection Experience or the full Immersion Retreat.

Together, the three experiences form **The Sounds Joyful Workplace Wellbeing Programme**:

- **Energise** → The Reset Experience
- **Connect** → The Connection Experience  
- **Reset** → The Immersion Retreat

Companies can book individual sessions or design a full programme.
