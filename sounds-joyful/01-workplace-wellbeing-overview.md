## Introduction

Modern workplaces are fast-paced, demanding environments where stress and disconnection can quietly build over time.

Research shows that only one in five employees are truly thriving at work, while nearly 60% report feeling stressed most of the time.

At the same time, the strongest drivers of wellbeing at work are not salary or perks — but belonging, energy and meaningful human connection.

**This is where music becomes powerful.**

At Sounds Joyful, we create immersive musical experiences that bring people together quickly and authentically.

Through rhythm, voice and deep listening, teams experience something rare in the modern workplace: genuine collective energy.

People arrive tired, distracted or cautious.

They leave feeling lighter, connected and re-energised.

No musical experience is required. Just a willingness to take part.

## Social Impact

Sounds Joyful is a Community Interest Company.

Profits from our workplace programmes directly support our community music sessions, which bring people of all ages and abilities together — including those living with dementia, learning disabilities and complex additional needs.

By working with Sounds Joyful, your organisation is not only investing in the wellbeing of your team but also helping to build stronger, more inclusive communities.
