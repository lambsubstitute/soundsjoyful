## The Problem You Solve

Team away days and training sessions often fall flat.

People sit through presentations. They do icebreakers. They leave with handouts and good intentions — but little has actually shifted.

What teams need is something that creates real connection: shared experience, vulnerability, laughter and the kind of trust that only comes when people create something together.

---

## What the Experience Is Like

This dynamic three-hour experience brings teams together through the universal language of music.

Participants move through a carefully structured series of:

- **Rhythm experiences** — Drumming and collective percussion
- **Collective voice experiences** — Singing together (no performance required)
- **Immersive sound experiences** — Deep listening and relaxation
- **Reflection and insight** — Jo and Emma share powerful perspectives on connection, belonging and collective energy

The result is an experience that is energising, thought-provoking and genuinely memorable.

Teams often leave with a renewed sense of cohesion, energy and shared purpose — and practical insights they can carry back into their everyday working lives.

---

## Why Music Works

Group singing and rhythm-making:

- Release oxytocin — the bonding hormone
- Create shared emotional states — we literally sync up
- Build trust faster than traditional team-building exercises
- Activate creativity and collaborative thinking

Think Simon Sinek — but with a practical tool (music) that makes the magic happen without anyone having to try.
